Stoichiometry

Stoichiometry is the math involved in calculating proportions of chemical reactants and chemical products in any given reaction.

To begin performing stoichiometry, many concepts need to be known. The foremost concept is balancing chemical equations. When two chemicals react, they can react in unequal proportions.
